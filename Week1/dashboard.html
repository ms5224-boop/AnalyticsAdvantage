🎯
Target High-History Customers First
Customers with a High purchase history spend an average of $2,186 — 6× more than Low-history customers ($357). Prioritise retention and upsell campaigns for this cohort.
📮
Increase Catalog Send Volume
Moving customers from 6 catalogs to 24 nearly triples average spend ($617 → $1,828). A/B test incremental catalog increases for cost-effective revenue growth.
💑
Married Homeowners Are Premium Segment
Married customers average $1,672 vs $758 for single. Homeowners spend $1,543 vs $869 for renters. Combined, this segment likely represents your highest LTV group.
💰
Salary Is a Strong Spend Predictor
Customers earning $100k+ spend an average of $2,442 — 8× more than those earning under $20k ($302). Enrich CRM data with income signals for better prospect scoring.
📍
Far-Location Customers Outspend Close
Far-location customers average $1,596 vs $1,062 for close. This may reflect fewer local alternatives — consider geo-targeted direct mail for distant zip codes.
👶
Re-engage Young & High-Children Customers
Young customers average only $559. Customers with 3 children average $831. These cost-constrained groups need value-focused messaging and entry-level offers.
